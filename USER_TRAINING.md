# DMS Pro - User Training Document

Welcome to **DMS Pro**, the next-generation Distribution Management System designed for modern FMCG distribution. This guide will help you navigate and master the platform.

---

## 1. Getting Started
### 1.1 Login
- Access the application via the provided URL.
- Click **"Sign in with Google"**.
- Use your corporate Google account to authenticate.
- **Note:** First-time users are assigned the 'Sales Representative' role by default. Admins can upgrade roles in the 'User Management' section.

### 1.2 Dashboard Overview
The Dashboard provides a real-time snapshot of your operations:
- **Operational Alerts:** Low stock warnings, credit limit breaches, and upcoming expiries.
- **Key Metrics:** Today's Sales, Pending Orders, Out of Stock SKUs, and Low Stock SKUs.
- **Recent Activity:** A log of the latest orders and their statuses.

---

## 2. Inventory Management
### 2.1 Viewing Products
- Navigate to **"Product Master"** in the sidebar.
- Use the **Fuzzy Search** bar to find products by SKU, Name, or Brand.
- Filter by **Brand/Company** (e.g., HUL, ITC, Amul) using the dropdown.

### 2.2 Adding New Products
- Click **"Add Product"** in the Product Master view.
- Fill in the SKU, Name, Brand, Category, and Pricing details.
- Set the **Reorder Level** to receive automatic low-stock alerts.

### 2.3 Stock Updates
- Click on any product to view its **Product Detail Page**.
- Click **"Update Stock"** to add or adjust inventory.
- Every stock change is automatically logged in the **"Stock Movement"** section for auditing.

---

## 3. Order Management
### 3.1 Booking an Order
- Click the **"Quick Order"** button (available in Dashboard or Orders view).
- **Step 1:** Select a Customer.
- **Step 2:** Choose Order Type (Regular, Urgent, Sample, Return).
- **Step 3:** Add products to the cart. The system checks real-time stock availability.
- **Step 4:** Review and Place Order.
- **Note:** Orders exceeding credit limits or ₹50,000 require Manager Approval.

### 3.2 Order Tracking
- Navigate to **"Order Management"**.
- View orders by status: Pending, Dispatched, Delivered, or On Hold.
- Click on an order to view the **Digital Invoice**.

---

## 4. Warehouse & Logistics
### 4.1 Stock Movement Log
- Navigate to **"Stock Movement"**.
- View every transaction (GRN, Sales, Transfers, Adjustments) with timestamps and reasons.

### 4.2 Picking Lists
- Navigate to **"Picking List"**.
- Generate and view lists for pending orders to streamline warehouse operations.

---

## 5. Administration (Super Admin Only)
### 5.1 User Management
- Navigate to **"User Management"**.
- View all registered users.
- Upgrade or downgrade user roles (e.g., promote a Sales Rep to Sales Manager).

### 5.2 Company Master
- Manage multiple principals (HUL, ITC, etc.) and their specific configurations.

---

## 6. Support & Troubleshooting
- **Stock not updating?** Ensure you are using the "Update Stock" button or placing orders through the portal.
- **Missing Sidebar Items?** Your role may not have permission to view certain modules. Contact your Super Admin.
- **Login Issues?** Ensure you are using a valid Google account.
